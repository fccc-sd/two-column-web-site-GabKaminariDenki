The Different Classes or Occupations in DnD


There are so much more races, and there are different classes or occupation that your character can do. Here's a list of some of those classes nad what the classes are about.

  • - Druid: belongs to the ancient orders that call on forces of nature, as a Druid your magic comes from the nature around you.

  • - Cleric: As a Cleric you draw your power from the realms of the gods and harness it to work mircacles, you can be blessed by a deity, pantheon, or another immortal entity.

  • - Rogue: As a Rogue you rely on cunning, stealth, and your foe's vunlerabilites to get the upper hand of a situation. You may even have learned a few magical tricks to help your abilites as a Rogue.

  • - Warlock: As a Warlock your quest for knowlegde lies hidden in the fabric of the multiverse, you get your magic from your patrons, some warlocks respects theirs while others do not.

There are so much more with the classes as well like the races and each class has different morals or want of something, nad its fun as the player to act out as your DnD character and show the other players and the DM (Dungeon Master) how your character reacts to situations and other things too.
